Gridley™

I needed a utility that could quickly convert a handful of images into a simple, single "grid" image. After looking around and not finding exactly what I wanted I just made something myself ... which I've named Gridley™. It's a very simple, but powerful, utility that can save a lot of time in a variety of situations:

  • Creating Documention/Tutorials
  • Support Portal Ticket Screenshots
  • Comparing Design Mockups
  • Consolidating Photos
  • ... and many more!

Try out Gridley™ today and see if you find it useful too!
